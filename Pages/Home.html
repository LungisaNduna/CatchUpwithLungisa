Welcome to Catching Up With Lungisa.

My name is Lungisa and I am doing a virtual catch up session with the masses

This is primarily based on a request by my mother to communicate with some family members and an internal guilt that I have not done so in some time.

So this site is very simple. There are some links which will take you to relevant information about my life and those close to me.

This is not a site I will be updating regularly so once you have been here it is unlikely that there will be new things to see at a later stage. But I hope you got a lot out of it
